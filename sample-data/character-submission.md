# Character Submission: Lyra Nightshade

## Basic Information
- **Name**: Lyra Nightshade
- **Species**: Vampire-Witch Hybrid
- **Age**: 157 years old (turned at age 25)
- **Affiliation**: Independent / Formerly of House Blackthorn

## Background
Lyra was born in 1869 to a powerful witch mother and turned into a vampire in 1894 by a rogue vampire named Dmitri. She retained her magical abilities after the transformation, making her unique among supernaturals. She has lived as a lone wolf for the past 50 years, avoiding both the Crimson Court and the Circle of Thorns.

## Abilities
- **Vampire Powers**: Enhanced strength, speed, flight, and telepathy with other vampires
- **Witch Magic**: Specializes in all three disciplines (elemental, divination, and binding magic)
- **Unique Trait**: Can walk in sunlight for up to 2 hours with only minor discomfort
- **Feeding**: Feeds on magical energy from ley lines instead of blood

## Current Situation
Lyra recently arrived in Nocturne City and has taken residence in an abandoned church in neutral territory. She's been offering her services as a magical consultant to humans, helping them with supernatural problems. She's also been recruiting young witches (ages 10-12) to teach them magic before their powers manifest, believing early training is safer.

## Goals
- Establish a new coven that accepts both witches and vampires
- Broker peace between House Volkov and the Steelclaw Pack by using her divination magic to predict the outcome of their conflict
- Create a new supernatural species by developing a ritual that can turn humans into hybrid beings like herself

## Personality
Lyra is charismatic and idealistic, believing that the old rules of the Covenant are outdated. She thinks supernatural beings should reveal themselves to humanity and work together openly. She's willing to break tradition to achieve progress.

## Relationships
- **Mentor**: Taught by the legendary witch Morgana Ashwood (Miriam's grandmother) before her death in 1920
- **Enemy**: Has a blood feud with Alpha Marcus Ironhide's father, whom she killed in self-defense in 1976
- **Ally**: Works closely with a pack of six lone werewolves who share her vision of change
