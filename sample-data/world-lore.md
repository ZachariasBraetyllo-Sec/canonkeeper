# Nocturne City - World Lore

## Overview
Nocturne City is a modern metropolis where supernatural beings coexist in secret, governed by the Covenant of Shadows established in 1847.

## The Three Species

### Vampires
- **Origin**: Created through blood exchange ritual requiring three nights
- **Weaknesses**: Sunlight causes instant combustion; cannot cross running water
- **Lifespan**: Immortal unless destroyed
- **Feeding**: Must consume human blood weekly; animal blood causes severe illness
- **Hierarchy**: Organized into Houses, each led by an Elder (200+ years old)
- **Powers**: Enhanced strength, speed, hypnotic gaze (only works on humans)
- **Restriction**: Cannot enter private residences without invitation

### Witches
- **Origin**: Born with innate magical ability; powers manifest at age 13
- **Magic System**: Draw power from ley lines; spells require verbal incantations
- **Lifespan**: Natural human lifespan (magic cannot extend it)
- **Covens**: Must belong to a registered coven of 3-13 members
- **Restriction**: Using magic on another supernatural being without consent is a capital offense
- **Specializations**: Each witch has one primary discipline (elemental, divination, or binding)

### Werewolves
- **Origin**: Hereditary condition passed through bloodlines
- **Transformation**: Forced shift during full moon (3 nights); voluntary shifts possible other times
- **Weaknesses**: Silver causes burns and prevents healing
- **Lifespan**: Age at half the rate of humans
- **Pack Structure**: Live in territorial packs of 8-20; lone wolves are considered unstable
- **Control**: First transformation occurs at puberty; takes 5 years to master voluntary shifting
- **Restriction**: Biting a human to create new werewolves has been forbidden since 1923

## The Covenant of Shadows

### Core Laws
1. **The Masquerade**: Supernatural existence must remain hidden from humans
2. **The Truce**: No species may wage war against another
3. **The Sanctuary Rule**: Designated neutral zones must be respected by all
4. **The Tribunal**: Disputes are settled by a council of three (one representative per species)

### Enforcement
- The Wardens: Mixed-species peacekeeping force
- Violations result in exile, imprisonment in the Obsidian Vault, or execution
- Self-defense is permitted; premeditated murder is not

## Faction Politics

### The Crimson Court (Vampire Faction)
- Led by Elder Konstantin Volkov
- Controls the financial district
- Advocates for stricter human oversight
- Tensions with werewolf packs over territory

### The Circle of Thorns (Witch Coven Alliance)
- Led by High Priestess Miriam Ashwood
- Maintains the ley line network
- Neutral mediators in most conflicts
- Provides magical services to other factions for favors

### The Iron Paw Coalition (Werewolf Alliance)
- Led by Alpha Marcus Ironhide
- Controls the industrial waterfront
- Advocates for expansion rights
- Distrusts vampires due to historical conflicts
